Helen Skelton shocked the whole of Great Britain with her statements. Nobody expected this!

  • Published
Share page
About sharing
By Jane Wakefield
Business reporter

During the live broadcast of the show "Loose Women", a serious scandal broke out between the host Kay Adams and the guest Helen Skelton. During the heated discussion, Kay Adams questioned the truthfulness of Helen Skelton's words, calling her a liar right in front of thousands of live viewers. In response to the incident, the TV channel's management decided to remove the interview from the air, refusing to comment on the situation and the behavior of its anchor Kay Adams.

DESPITE ALL THE DIFFICULTIES, OUR EDITORIAL STAFF DECIDED TO PUBLISH THIS INTERVIEW:

Kay Adams: "You practically don't work - I will never believe that you live on one salary! A year ago you were driving an old car and living in a small apartment. How did you manage to change all that?"

Helen Skelton: "Just a year ago? And for me it was like a past life! Now I live in a country cottage, a month ago I bought an apartment to rent out, and I got rid of my old car a long time ago - now I drive a new Land Rover. But I work a lot, so don't accuse me of being lazy. I understand your surprise, but I advise you to start living differently".

Kay Adams: "It's easy for you to give out advice! And I have to suffer every day at work to feed my family. And you know what? I'm always short of money anyway".

Helen Skelton: "What's the problem with starting to think differently? Just accept the idea that work is not the only way to make money. When someone tells me that I'm just lucky - I laugh in their face because today there's everything on the Internet that makes it easy to get rich without getting off the couch".

Kay Adams: "So you're saying that everyone can make money like you? I don't believe what you're saying..."

After this sentence, Helen Skelton became furious. She started to argue with Kay Adams while showing her something on her phone.

Helen Skelton: "You don't believe me? Give me 200 pounds and I'll make a million with it in 3-4 months! It all depends on you - whether you want it or not".

Kay Adams:"All the viewers saw that you showed me the website where you make money, the camera captured the link! Why would you do that on live TV? It could harm the state economy!"

Helen Skelton: "Don't make me a monster, I want to do my best and open people's eyes to the truth. Why would anyone be bothered by the extra income?"

Kay Adams:"Think about it - if everyone starts getting rich, who would work in hospitals, schools, and factories? What would you say to that?"

Helen Skelton: "I think people should have a choice in how they earn their money - by working hard all their lives or by spending an hour a day sitting in bed. If you don't believe what I say, see for yourself. Give me your phone and I will register you on the platform. Let the live viewers understand that this method truly works and passive income is available to anyone".

Kay Adams handed over her smartphone, whereupon Helen Skelton registered an account for Ben Shephard on the platform at this link. Five minutes later, Helen Skelton handed the phone back to Kay Adams.

Helen Skelton: "I just registered you on the platform and made a minimum deposit - it is only 200 pounds. Let's check in half an hour how much money you were able to earn passively on this platform".

Kay Adams: "Okay, let's check it out. But how much can you really earn from it? And what about people for whom 200 pounds is a large sum? Or do you think everyone has that money?"

Helen Skelton: "When I found out about Wealthy Gainer Ai, I didn't have any extra money myself because we were going through a pandemic and there were hardly any paychecks. I had to use my old credit card and that's where it all started".

The next half hour of the interview was spent discussing and reminiscing about working in TV during the pandemic. After a long conversation, Helen Skelton returned to the topic of passive income.

Helen Skelton: "Let's check your account! How much did you manage to earn in those 30 minutes while we were chatting?"

Kay Adams opened an account on the platform and was amazed. In just 30 minutes the program made 3 trades: one of them brought in a small income, but the other two made good profits. The balance grew from 200 pounds to 297 pounds.

Kay Adams: "47 pounds net profit! That's incredible!"

Helen Skelton: "Now imagine how much money will be on the balance sheet in a month. In just one month, those 200 pounds can turn into 10,000 or even 12,000 pounds! All you have to do is register via this link, wait for a call from the platform manager to activate your account and deposit 200 pounds".

Kay Adams: "That sounds interesting! But I'm still not sure that this information should be available to everyone..."

Helen Skelton: "That's just your opinion, keep it to yourself. I don't feel bad about sharing this information if it can help other people".

If you are fortunate enough to read this article, please be aware that it may soon be deleted, as it happened with this telecast. So, save yourself the official link to Wealthy Gainer Ai as soon as possible, that was provided by Helen Skelton herself for our editorial team.
